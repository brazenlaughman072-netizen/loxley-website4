Loxley Historical Timeline

1870s

Founding Lumber Camp

In the early 1870s, Michigan native John Loxley established a lumber camp that grew into the Loxley community. Many workers who came with Loxley remained, forming the early settlement. 1906

Railroad Depot & Post Office
